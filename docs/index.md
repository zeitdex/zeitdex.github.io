# Zeitdex

**Community resources for circadian rhythm disorders (CRDs)** — including Non-24-Hour Sleep-Wake Disorder and the delayed and advanced sleep phase disorders.

Zeitdex collects plain-language explanations, practical tracking guidance, and links, maintained by and for people living with CRDs. It is the documentation companion to the **[Zeitlog](https://zeitlog.github.io/)** tracker.

!!! warning "Not medical advice"
    Zeitdex is community-maintained information, not medical advice. Always discuss your own situation with a qualified clinician.

## Start here

- **New to circadian rhythms?** Read [Circadian rhythm & zeitgebers](basics/circadian-rhythm.md).
- **Looking into Non-24?** See the [Non-24 overview](conditions/non-24.md).
- **Want to track your own rhythm?** See [Using Zeitlog](tracking/using-zeitlog.md).
- **Want to help?** Anyone can [contribute](contributing.md) — every page is editable via a pull request.

## Why "Zeitdex"?

From *zeitgeber* — German for "time-giver," the external cues (light, dark, routine) that set the body clock — plus *index*. The sibling tracker is **Zeitlog**.
